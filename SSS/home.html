Description:

In this game, players will develop the storyline through making different choices from the perspective of Tartaglia. Please make your choices carefully to achieve your desired ending.

The world at the depths of the abyss is also a manifestation of the thoughts of those who have experienced it. If you meet an old friend, please don't panic. All encounters are but a long awaited reunion.

Just, don’t indulge too much in beautiful dreams or you might drown.

Thus, we wish you a pleasant journey.


Tartaglia never tried to conceal the fact that he had learned from the Abyss. Anyone who meets him for the first time would find it difficult to connect him to the Abyss, be it the warriors from his hometown of lively frost and snow, or even to himself; the experiences that he has yet to share with others has blended into a dream of half-truths and lies.

If there ever comes a day where your past catches up to you--

Just joking, even if he is entrusted by others, how about revisiting that old place?
